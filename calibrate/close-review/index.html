OFL — Facilitation close review (demo)

DEMO — this is a live preview of the Open Facilitation Library close-review labeling surface. The turns below are invented for the demo, not real sessions. To label a real calibration pack, message your OFL coordinator.

For each closing moment, is this a good close? Mark Pass or Fail and give one line on why, in your own words. Trust your practice. There are no trick questions, and disagreement with the stated criterion is welcome.

What happens with your answers on real packs: they become teaching examples for an automated quality reviewer (advisory only; it flags, it never certifies or blocks anything), and they feed the Open Facilitation Library standard. Your name is not attached unless you want credit.

This demo takes about 5 minutes. Your progress saves in the browser automatically. When done, press "Export labels.json" to see the file that would come back on a real pack.

Optional: anything you would change about a criterion itself?
